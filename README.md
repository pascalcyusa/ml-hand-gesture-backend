# ML Hand Gesture

**ML Hand Gesture** is a web application for training and using machine learning models to play piano notes and control your LEGO Spike Prime motors.

## Key Features

- **Train in Browser**: Teach the AI to recognize your specific hand gestures using your webcam.
- **Piano Player**: Map your gestures to piano notes and play music in real-time.
- **LEGO Spike Prime Integration**: Control motors and build interactive projects by connecting to your LEGO hub via Bluetooth.
- **Community Models**: Share your trained models or import models created by others.

## Open Source & Privacy

This project is open source and designed with privacy in mind.

- **100% Client-Side**: All machine learning training and inference happens directly in your browser.
- **Private Data**: When you train a model, your camera data definitions never leave your device.
